About Minecraft

Minecraft is an open world game where the only thing limiting you is your own creativity and what you make of it! Minecraft is a game all about the blocky graphics and exploring. It's also one of the most customizable games on the market meaning you can essentially make Minecraft look and behave however you would like! Minecraft is a game created by "Notch" Markus Persson and was released on May 17th 2009.

Where to get Minecraft

Minecraft is avaliable to get on Minecraft.net and can be purchased after creating a Mojang or Microsoft Account. It's recommended to go with a Microsoft account because of the migration of Mojang accounts to Microsoft accounts. After you have made or logged in to your account you can click on the "Get Minecraft" button and follow the steps provided to buy it.
